Founded on the principles of organic movement and mindful presence, The Breath is more than a studio—it is a sanctuary for the modern soul to reconnect with its natural rhythm.

Our Story

What started as a small gathering in a sun-drenched attic has grown into a community of seekers. In 2016, our founder Elena sought to bridge the gap between rigorous athletic discipline and the profound silence of meditative practice.

The Breath was born from the belief that wellness is not a destination to be reached, but a state of being that is cultivated through consistency, community, and the courage to be still. Every corner of our space is intentionally designed to evoke the calm of the natural world.
